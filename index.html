About Us

Shaping Tomorrow's Leaders Today

Don Bosco Matric. Hr. Sec. School, located in the serene surroundings of Gundalapatti, Dharmapuri, stands as a beacon of academic excellence and holistic development in Tamil Nadu. Founded on the timeless values of Learn, Live & Lead, we nurture students into well-rounded individuals ready to face the world.

Our institution is affiliated with the Tamil Nadu State Board and follows a rigorous curriculum that balances academic rigor with extracurricular enrichment. We believe that true education goes beyond textbooks — it encompasses character building, sportsmanship, artistic expression, and a deep sense of responsibility towards society.

With a dedicated team of experienced educators, state-of-the-art facilities, and a nurturing environment, we provide every student with the tools they need to discover their potential. From the classroom to the playground, from science labs to cultural stages — Don Bosco is where young minds bloom.

Academic Excellence

Curriculum & Subject Groups

Don Bosco offers a robust and structured academic program aligned with the Tamil Nadu State Board syllabus. Our curriculum is thoughtfully designed to prepare students for competitive examinations and future higher education, with particular strength in Science and Mathematics.

We provide two distinct subject group combinations for higher secondary students, catering to diverse career aspirations — whether in pure sciences, engineering, computing, or beyond.
